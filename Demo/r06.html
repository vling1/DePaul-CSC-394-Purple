Strawberry Donuts

by Sarah G. Dahl

Cooking time: 40 min

Difficulty: ★★★★☆

For the donuts:

• 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

• 1/2 cup granulated sugar

• 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder

• 1/4 teaspoon baking soda

• 1/4 teaspoon salt

• 1/2 cup mashed strawberries (fresh or frozen, thawed)

• 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

• 1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)

• 1 large egg

• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract


For the glaze:

• 1 cup powdered sugar

• 2-3 tablespoons mashed strawberries (fresh or frozen, thawed)

• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract


Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a donut pan with non-stick cooking spray or butter.

2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

3. In another bowl, whisk together the mashed strawberries, melted butter, milk, egg, and vanilla extract until well combined.

4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix.

5. Spoon the batter into a piping bag or a large ziplock bag with the corner cut off. Pipe the batter into the prepared donut pan, filling each cavity about 2/3 full.

6. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the donuts spring back when lightly pressed.

7. Allow the donuts to cool in the pan for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

8. While the donuts are cooling, make the glaze by whisking together the powdered sugar, mashed strawberries, and vanilla extract until smooth.

9. Once the donuts are completely cooled, dip the tops of each donut into the glaze and return them to the wire rack to allow any excess glaze to drip off.

10. Let the glaze set for about 10-15 minutes before serving.

11. Enjoy your delightful strawberry donuts with a cup of coffee or tea!
