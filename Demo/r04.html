Honey Sriracha Wings

Sofia Kottila

Cooking time: 40 min

Difficulty: ★★★★☆

Ingredients:

• 2 pounds chicken wings, split at joints, tips discarded, and wings patted dry with paper towels

• Salt and black pepper, to taste

• 1/4 cup honey

• 2 tablespoons Sriracha sauce (adjust according to your spice preference)

• 2 tablespoons soy sauce

• 2 cloves garlic, minced

• 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced

• 1 tablespoon rice vinegar

• 2 tablespoons sesame oil

• Sesame seeds, for garnish (optional)

• Chopped green onions, for garnish (optional)


Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

2. Season the chicken wings with salt and black pepper.

3. Place the seasoned wings on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or aluminum foil.

4. Bake the wings in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy.

5. In a small saucepan, combine the honey, Sriracha sauce, soy sauce, minced garlic, minced ginger, rice vinegar, and sesame oil.

6. Cook the sauce over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until it starts to simmer. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes until slightly thickened.

7. Once the wings are cooked, transfer them to a large mixing bowl.

8. Pour the Honey Sriracha sauce over the wings and toss them gently until they are evenly coated.


Serving:

1. Transfer the glazed wings to a serving platter.

2. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions, if desired.

3. Serve hot and enjoy your Honey Sriracha Wings as a delicious appetizer or main dish.
